Garden Table Recipes

This name was chosen because the site will provide recipes based on fresh ingredients that can easily be grown in a personal garden.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to inspire users to grow a garden that can practically be used to cook meals for themselves and their families. It will ascertain what they are growing and what recipes they could cook up. Alternatively, it could work backwards with recipes they'd like to make and come up with a list of fruits, vegetables, and herbs they would need in their garden.

Scenarios

  • A person wants to start a garden but doesn't know what they should grow. They could come to this site to make that choice based on future meals they want to make
  • A user has a garden but wants ideas for what to do with their harvest. They can search recipes based on what they are currently growing.
  • User wants to keep track of their recipes or create their own to save them for future use with their home garden.
  • User wants to share pictures of their garden and the meals they made from it on social media
